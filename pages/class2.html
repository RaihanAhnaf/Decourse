Why Learn UI/UX Design?

The development of the tech industry and the emergence of many startups introduce us to several new job names. As in the design industry, there are new job categories called User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) designers. Basically, UI and UX are parts of a Web app or Mobile App which are included as products of a strartup. This line of work is closely related to product design. It's just that the product being developed is a digital product. The UI/UX Design process will be similar to the the product design process which certainly requires research to get a functional design result without reducing the beauty of an application.

Many startuos and technology companies that want to develop Web apps and Mobile apps certainly need a UI / UX Designer to develop applications in terms of functionality, aesthetics, and branding. The User Interface of an application will provide the identity of a strartup. While the User Experience of an application will give an impression and consumer behavior towards the product.
